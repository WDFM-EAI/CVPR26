Call for Papers

We invite submissions to the 1st Workshop on Deployment of Foundation Models for Embodied AI (WDFM-EAI) at CVPR 2026.

Important Dates

  • Paper Submission Deadline: TBD (Tentative: March 2026)
  • Notification of Acceptance: TBD (Tentative: April 2026)
  • Camera-Ready Deadline: TBD (Tentative: May 2026)
  • Workshop Date: June 2026 (co-located with CVPR 2026)

All dates are tentative and subject to change. Please check back for updates.

Topics of Interest

We welcome submissions on topics including, but not limited to:

  • Multimodal Perception for Embodied AI
  • Real-Time Decision Making with Foundation Models
  • Vision-Language-Action Models in Embodied AI Systems
  • World Models for Planning and Control
  • Model Compression and Distillation for Autonomous Systems
  • Robustness and Safety in Multimodal Autonomous Systems
  • Generative Models for Simulation and Training
  • Cross-Domain Applications of Foundation Models in Embodied Platforms

Submission Guidelines

Paper Format:

  • Papers should follow the CVPR 2026 format guidelines
  • Maximum length: 8 pages excluding references

Submission Process:

  • Submissions will be handled through OpenReview
  • All submissions must be anonymized for double-blind review
  • Accepted papers will be published in the workshop proceedings

Detailed submission instructions will be provided closer to the submission deadline.

Review Process

  • All submissions will undergo a double-blind peer review process
  • Each paper will be reviewed by at least 2-3 reviewers
  • Decisions will be based on reviewer recommendations and program committee discussion

Awards

We are planning to recognize outstanding contributions. Details will be announced closer to the workshop date.
